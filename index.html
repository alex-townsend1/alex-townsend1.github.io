About Me

Hello my name is Alex Townsend and I am a junior at the University of Maryland. I am a double major in criminology/criminal justice and journalism. I also have a minor in global terrorism studies. I am currently writing for Stories Beneath the Shell. A UMD news publication dedicated on covering more underreported stories. My interest in journalism stems from learning about current events abroad in other countries, specifically how journalists report in countries where free speech is not guaranteed. I want to combine my majors and interest and focus on captured journalists. It's a critical, high-stakes area that lets me explore everything from the ethics of reporting in conflict zones to the psychological impact of captivity and the failures of international law to protect the press. Apart from journalism I am a University Tour Guide, showing off the campus to hundreds of prospective new students.
